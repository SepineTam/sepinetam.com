About

I am an undergraduate student at Shanghai University, majoring in Economics. My research focuses on Labor Economics, Development Economics, and AI applications in economic research.

I am the founder of Stata-MCP, an open-source tool that enables LLMs to write and execute Stata code automatically. I also serve as CEO at Shanghai Bayes Views Info-tech Co., Ltd., working on AI-powered accessibility solutions.

Beyond research, I create content about economics through my podcast "Lunjin: Economics in Depth".

Research Interests

  • Labor Economics (gender equality, labor markets, employment in China)
  • Development Economics (rural development, cooperatives, sustainable growth)
  • Economic History (long-term development patterns, institutional change)
  • AI in Economics (automated empirical analysis, LLM-assisted research)
